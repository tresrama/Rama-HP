Stagedork has been the exclusive publication for Philippine theatregoers, providing complete cast and production credits for each show, as well as features and interviews highlighting the many voices that make up the theatre community. Stagedork proudly serves Philippines' most prominent performing arts venues, including multiple venues at Solaire Theater, the Meralco Theater,Newport Performing Arts Theater, the Metropolitan Theater, and more; as well as theatres across the country, including the Hollywood Pantages Theatre in Los Angeles and the Goodman Theatre in Chicago.

Stagedork has expanded beyond a print magazine and established itself as a trusted name in the theatre industry, offering audiences new ways to connect to Broadway and Off-Broadway around the world via Stagedork.com. With over 3 million unique visitors, Stagedork.com is the preeminent site for theatre news and features, delivering high-quality, original content to audiences everywhere. Over the past two decades, Stagedork has also launched StageStore.com, the one stop Broadway shop for theatre fans, carrying the official souvenir merchandise of Playbill and Broadway shows; PLAYBILLder, which allows school and community theatre productions around the world to build their very own Broadway quality Stagedork program; PlayDork Vault, a comprehensive online database of Broadway history that provides records of Broadway productions from 1930 to the present; and Stagedork Travel, which takes travelers and Broadway royalty around the globe to experience the sights, sounds and tastes of the world’s finest cultures, while offering world-class concerts at night.
